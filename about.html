About Oliver & Co

Leading indigenous brand in dredging and metal manufacturing, recognized for innovation, reliability, and sustainable impact

Who We Are

Oliver & Co Metal Works is a multifaceted company with expertise in metal works, construction, and related services. We specialize in designing and manufacturing dredging machines that cater to the diverse needs of our clients in the maritime and construction industries.

Our company operates in four key phases: Construction of Dredgers, Services Maintenance and Repair, Haulage Business, and Sales of Building Materials and Construction. This comprehensive approach allows us to serve our clients throughout their entire project lifecycle.

At Oliver & Co Metal Works, we're committed to delivering exceptional quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction in all our endeavors. We strive to be a trusted partner for our clients, providing innovative solutions and value-added services that meet their evolving needs.

Our Leadership

Meet the visionary driving our success

Mr. Oliver

Managing Director / Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Oliver is a dynamic and visionary business leader with extensive experience in the dredging, metal fabrication, and haulage sectors. With over a decade of progressive experience in heavy-duty equipment operations, sand dredging, and logistics management, he has successfully positioned Oliver & Co Metal Work Ltd as a trusted name in Nigeria's construction and marine service industry.

His managerial style is defined by his strong commitment to efficiency, safety, and customer satisfaction. He emphasizes professionalism, teamwork, and continuous improvement, ensuring that every project aligns with international best practices and environmental standards.
