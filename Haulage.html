Haulage Services

Reliable transportation of heavy equipment and materials for construction and maritime sectors with modern fleet and experienced logistics team.

Professional Heavy Equipment Transport

Oliver & Co Metal Works offers reliable haulage services, specializing in transporting heavy equipment and materials for the construction and maritime sectors.

Our services cater to the specific needs of these industries, ensuring timely and efficient delivery of goods. We maintain a modern fleet of well-maintained HOWO trucks operated by experienced drivers.

From dredging equipment to building materials, our logistics expertise ensures safe transport and on-time delivery across Nigeria.
